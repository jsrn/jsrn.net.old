The Things I Use

When I'm at the office, I do most of my development work with RubyMine via a very snazzy UK 104 key mechanical keyboard from WASD Keyboards.

The machine itself is a cheap and cheerful Windows 10 desktop from eBuyer or something (I don't know, the company got it), and I use docker with debian images for development.

At home or on the move, I use an ageing HP G62 laptop. It's dark red and it's served me well for a long time. It's too slow for RubyMine, so I use the remarkable Sublime Text.
